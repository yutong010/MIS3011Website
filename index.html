Course Overview

This course is designed for those ready to explore the cutting-edge technology of artificial intelligence from a business perspective. The course spans 14 intricately designed weeks that merge theory with hands-on experience.

AI Fundamentals

Unravel AI's constituents and understand the core principles that drive artificial intelligence systems.

Practical Applications

Gain hands-on experience through lab sessions and real-world case studies to apply AI in business contexts.

Business Innovation

Learn how AI can transform business landscapes and explore ethics and regulations guiding AI deployment.

Learning Outcomes

Core AI Concepts

Understand AI principles, algorithms, data management, machine learning, and neural networks

Algorithmic Thinking

Apply algorithmic thinking and programming skills in AI-based business solutions

Data Analytics

Gain proficiency in hands-on use of data processing and analytic tools for AI solutions

Business Applications

Understand the business implications and opportunities of AI implementation

Solution Evaluation

Evaluate the benefits and limitations of different AI solutions in business contexts

Ethical Considerations

Know the ethical considerations and potential risks associated with AI deployment
